How We Work

Your Sourcing Journey

Sourcing to Shipping to Support. We've got you covered — every step of the way.

1
Phase 01
Requirement Analysis

Active consultations to gather key project information and suggest the right solutions from our product matrix.

2
Phase 02
Sourcing

Sourcing products and suppliers for your project, getting the best quotations. Handheld guidance on sourcing markets and products.

3
Phase 03
Project Coordination

Assist clients during market visits to China. Aiding negotiations, translations, and follow-up with suppliers after purchase.

4
Phase 04
Quality Check

Thorough inspection of materials during production, after production and before shipment depending on project requirements.

5
Phase 05
Container Planning

Consolidation of materials, planning based on weight and cubic meters, arranging containers to optimize shipping costs.

6
Phase 06
Loading of Materials

Container-wise planning and management for loading materials into the assigned containers with precision.

7
Phase 07
Shipping & Logistics

Execute logistical requirements from China to destination — best shipping lines, thorough export documentation, country-specific support.

8
Phase 08
Support

Last-mile services for product placement, installation, and all relevant after-sales coordination and support.
