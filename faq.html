We quote FOB, CFR or DDP with line-item breakdowns. Our service fee is transparent and flat-rate for sourcing projects, or a percentage on full turnkey engagements — agreed upfront in writing.

Standard industry terms: 30% deposit on PO, 70% before shipment. We accept bank wire (T/T), LC for large orders, and escrow through verified third parties. We do not accept payment to personal accounts.

Quality & Compliance

Every new supplier goes through a 12-point audit: business license verification, facility walk-through, capacity assessment, QC system review, sample testing and reference checks. We then monitor with pre-production, in-process and pre-shipment inspections.
